Executive Summary

FounderSearching.com is a platform aimed at connecting founders with collaborators using AI-powered matchmaking, gamification, and community-driven invitations. Our goal is to provide a democratic way to form partnerships and accelerate startup development. We are partnered with FlipIt and Made With Chat to enhance our features and reach a wider audience.

Target Audience

Our target audience includes founders seeking partners and collaborators looking to join new projects. We specifically focus on connecting those who have ideas but lack the team to execute them with talented individuals who want to contribute to meaningful projects but haven't found the right opportunity.

Value Proposition

FounderSearching.com offers efficient matchmaking, gamified interactions, and a community-driven environment to help users connect and collaborate effectively. Our platform replaces traditional gatekeepers with network effects and emphasizes reputation over resumes, creating opportunities for all participants regardless of their background.

Marketing Strategy

Our marketing strategy includes leveraging partnerships with FlipIt and Made With Chat, community-driven invitations, gamification, and a referral program. By incentivizing early adopters to invite others and rewarding network-building activities, we create a self-sustaining growth engine.

Revenue Model

Our revenue model includes tiered subscription plans ranging from free to $25/month, potential revenue sharing with Made With Chat for successful startups, and a founding member program that drives both engagement and revenue.

Funding Needs

We are currently raising ten thousand dollars by offering twenty percent equity to two investors to launch stage one and build the network. This initial funding will support platform development, marketing efforts, and operational costs during our beta phase.

Milestones

We aim to launch the platform in less than two months, achieve viral growth through our referral system, and enable the swiping functionality within the second or third month. Our long-term milestones include reaching 100,000 users within the first year and facilitating the formation of at least 1,000 startup teams.

Team

Our core team consists of experts in FlipIt, Made With Chat, and FounderSearching.com, with plans to hire expert developers and utilize AI coding. We are building a lean, agile team that can quickly adapt to user feedback and market demands.

Market Analysis

The global market for online recruitment and professional networking platforms is currently valued at over $33 billion and is projected to exceed $60 billion within the next decade. Co-founder and early-team matching is an emerging but underserved segment within this massive market.

Founder Searching addresses this gap by focusing on collaborative partnerships over traditional hiring. Unlike generalist platforms like LinkedIn, or closed networks like Y Combinator's matching system, our approach emphasizes intent-based, AI-driven matching focused on equity, purpose, and skill alignment.

Key Market Drivers

Digital Transformation

The shift toward online matchmaking platforms has been a significant driver of growth across all segments of the matchmaking industry. Founder Searching is positioned to capitalize on this trend by providing a digital platform specifically designed for startup ecosystem connections.

AI Matching

Advanced matchmaking algorithms and artificial intelligence technologies have enabled more accurate and personalized matching across various industries. Founder Searching leverages these technologies to increase the likelihood of successful connections.

Professional Networking

The matchmaking industry has expanded beyond romantic relationships to include professional networking. Founder Searching is positioned at the forefront of this trend, focusing specifically on connecting entrepreneurs with potential team members.

Competitor Analysis

The founder matchmaking space includes several established and emerging platforms, each with distinct approaches and value propositions:

Y Combinator's platform has facilitated over 100,000 co-founder introductions, making it one of the largest players in this space. As part of a prestigious accelerator that has funded over 5,000 startups with a combined valuation of approximately $600 billion, YC's platform benefits from strong credibility and brand recognition. However, it has limitations in filtering options and variable quality of matches.

With over 650,000 users worldwide, CoFoundersLab is one of the largest co-founder matchmaking services. It offers a comprehensive platform for starting, funding, and growing businesses, with access to investors and professionals. While it provides extensive resources, its subscription model and reported customer service issues present opportunities for differentiation.

Founder Searching is uniquely positioned in this market by focusing specifically on connecting founders with collaborators who might otherwise never find each other, emphasizing mission alignment and purpose over traditional resume-based matching, and creating a community-driven approach with network effects and incentives.
